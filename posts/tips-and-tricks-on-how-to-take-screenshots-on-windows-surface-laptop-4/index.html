1. Step-by-step guide on how to use the in-built screenshot function on Windows Surface Laptop 4#

Capturing high-quality screenshots on your Windows Surface Laptop 4 has never been easier. This handy step-by-step guide will take you through the process of using the in-built screenshot function. First, open the image you wish to screenshot. Then, press the PrtScn button located above the delete key. This captures the entire screen image. To take a screenshot of only the active window, press Alt and PrtScn at the same time. To capture the active window, press Alt and Fn and PrtScn all at the same time. The screenshot will be automatically saved as a .png file to the Pictures folder located in the File Explorer. You can view or edit this image in any image editing program. With these simple steps, you can quickly and easily capture a screenshot on your Windows Surface Laptop 4.

a. Taking a Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot on a Windows Surface Laptop 4 is fairly simple. Firstly, press the Windows logo button on the bottom left of the laptop’s keyboard, and simultaneously press the Volume Down button. This combination of buttons will activate and take the screenshot. The screenshot will be stored in the Pictures folder of the laptop. To access the image, open the File Explorer and navigate to the Pictures folder. A screenshot will be the first image visible in that folder. To quickly access the screenshot, right click the image, then click ‘Open File Location’ to be taken to the folder. Lastly, the image can be edited, saved, and shared easily.

b. Editing and Sharing#

Taking screenshots on the Windows Surface Laptop 4 is surprisingly easy. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started. Firstly, locate the Print Screen button on your laptop. It is usually located at the top right corner of your keyboard. Once you have found it, press it and your laptop screen will flash to indicate that the screenshot is taken. The screenshot will be automatically saved to the Screenshots folder in your Pictures folder. To access the folder, open the File Explorer and go to the Pictures folder. There you will find the Screenshots folder, which contains all the screenshots you have taken. Finally, you can easily share the screenshot with your friends and family by simply right-clicking on the screenshot and selecting “Share”. With this simple guide, you can now take screenshots on your Windows Surface Laptop 4 with ease.
