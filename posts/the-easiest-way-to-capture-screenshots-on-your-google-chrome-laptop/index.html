1. What is a Screenshot#

A screenshot is an image taken of whatever is on your screen. This can be helpful for sharing what’s on your display, such as a website, with someone else or for creating visual aids for tutorials and presentations. Thankfully, it’s very easy to take a screenshot of your Google Chrome laptop. All you need to do is press the “Ctrl” and “Print Screen” buttons at the same time. This will capture the entire screen and save it to the clipboard. To access the image, simply open the Paint application from the Windows start menu and paste it in. From there, you can edit the image or save it as a file. If you’d like to capture just a select portion of the screen, press the “Ctrl”, “Shift”, and “Print Screen” buttons at the same time. A crosshair will appear, which you can use to select the area of the screen you’d like to capture. When you’re finished, the image will automatically be saved to the clipboard. Lastly, if you’d like to take a screenshot of the active window, press the “Alt” and “Print Screen” buttons at the same time. This will capture the active window and save it to the clipboard.
