The spell checker will now automatically check your spelling whenever you type something in a text field.

Another way to access the spell checker is through the Google Keyboard. To access this feature, open the Google Keyboard and then tap the ‘Settings’ icon. In the settings, choose the ‘Spell Checker’ option and then enable the option. The spell checker will now work whenever you type something in a text field.

If you are using a third-party keyboard, such as SwiftKey, the spell checker will be available through the keyboard’s settings. To activate the spell checker, open the keyboard’s settings and then choose the ‘Spell Check’ option. Once enabled, the spell checker will begin checking your spelling whenever you type something in a text field.

Finally, you can also manually check your spelling by using the ‘Check Spelling’ option in the menu. To use this option, select the text that you want to check and then tap the ‘Check Spelling’ icon. The spell checker will then scan your text and alert you of any spelling errors.
