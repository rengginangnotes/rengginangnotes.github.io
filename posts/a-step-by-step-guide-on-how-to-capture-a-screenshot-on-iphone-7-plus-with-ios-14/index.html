- Introduction to the Steps Needed to Capture a Screenshot#

Capturing a screenshot on your iPhone 7 Plus with iOS 14 is a simple task. All you need to do is press two buttons at the same time—the Volume Up and Side buttons—and a screenshot will be taken. To help you get started, here are the steps you need to follow:

First, locate the Volume Up and Side buttons on your device. The Side button is on the right side of the device and the Volume Up button is on the left side. Press and hold these two buttons at the same time.

Second, you should see a brief animation of the screen shrinking and a camera shutter sound. This indicates that the screenshot has been taken successfully. You can find the screenshot image in the Photos app.

Third, to get a better preview of the screenshot you’ve taken, you can tap the thumbnail that appears in the bottom left corner. This will open a preview of the screenshot.

Finally, to share the screenshot with your friends and family, you can tap the Share button. From there, you can select an app or contact that you’d like to send it to. With these four easy steps, you can easily capture screenshots on your iPhone 7 Plus with iOS 14.

Q1: How do I take a screenshot on my iPhone 7 Plus with iOS 14?

A1: To take a screenshot on an iPhone 7 Plus with iOS 14, press the Volume Up and the Side buttons simultaneously. The screenshot will be saved to the Photos app.

Q2: How can I access the screenshot I just captured?

A2: Once the screenshot is captured, it will be directly saved to the Photos app on your iPhone 7 Plus. You can access it by opening the Photos app and going to the Albums tab.

Q3: How do I edit the screenshot?

A3: You can easily edit the screenshot you just captured by opening it in the Photos app. In the editing screen, you can rotate, crop, edit colors, and apply filters to the screenshot.

Q4: How do I share the screenshot?

A4: In the Photos app, you can select the screenshot and tap on the Share button to share it with friends or family. You can also share the screenshot via social media, email, and other sharing apps.

Q5: How do I delete an unwanted screenshot?

A5: Open the Photos app and select the screenshot you want to delete. Tap on the Trash icon at the top of the screen to delete it. You can also delete unwanted screenshots in bulk by selecting multiple screenshots and tapping on the Trash icon.
