A2: You can clear the cache of your Android tablet in a few simple steps. First, navigate to your device’s Settings and select Apps. Select the app whose cache you want to clear and click on Storage. Q4: Will clearing the cache of my Android tablet delete any personal data?

A4: No, clearing the cache of your Android tablet will not delete any personal data. Cached data is generated by applications, which is different from personal data stored on the device.

Q5: What should I do if I can’t find the Clear Cache button on my Android tablet?

A5: If you can’t find the Clear Cache button on your Android tablet, make sure that you are running the latest version of the operating system. If you are on an Android version older than 7.0 Nougat, then you won’t be able to clear the cache.
