2. Recording Your Screen#

Android Moto devices are now widely used for a variety of purposes. Recording your screen on an Android Moto device is an important part of using it properly. Here are the steps to take for successful screen recording on an Android Moto device.

First, on the main home screen, select the “Settings” icon. Then, on the “Settings” page, scroll down and select “System”. Under the “System” section, you will find “Advanced”, then “Moto Shortcuts”.You should now be in “Moto Shortcuts” page. Here, swipe “Simultaneous Capture” to the “on” position.

Next, from the home screen select the “Display” icon. Then, select “Moto”. Under that, you will find “Moto Actions”. Select “Two Fingers Swipe”, and here activate “Screen Recording”.

Finally, to begin a screen recording, simply swipe two fingers from the top of the screen downward. This will open the recording dialogue box, where you can modify the resolution, frame rate, and quality of the recording. When you’re all set, select Start Recording and the recording will begin. When you’re finished, go ahead and select Stop Recording.

By following these steps, you should be able to record your screen on an Android Moto device quickly and easily.
