1. Enable the Screen Capture Feature#

Taking screenshot is one of the most basic features you can find on most Android smartphones. The Samsung A12 Android device is no exception, and provides a variety of tools to help users get the most out of this convenient feature. Here are the steps to enable the screen capture feature on Samsung A12 Android:

First, users must open the Settings app on their device. From there, select Advanced Features from the menu. Once in the Advanced Features menu, scroll down to the Smart Capture option and tap it to enable the feature.

Next, users must open the application or web page they want to take the screenshot of. Then they just have to drag down the notification bar and click on the Smart Capture icon. This will open up a menu of options, with which users can take a full-screen screenshot, take a picture of only the visible area or record a video of the screen.

Finally, users should select the option they need and click on the scissors icon on the right side of the menu to capture the image. The image will be saved in the Gallery app, ready for further use.

By following the directions outlined above, users can easily take and share screenshots on a Samsung A12 Android device.
