2. Introduction to QR Codes#

QR code, also known as Quick Response code, is a type of barcode which can be read by smartphones and other devices. It encodes text, URL, contacts, or other data that can be quickly scanned with a camera-equipped device, such as an Android smartphone. To scan a QR code with an Android camera, open the camera app on your phone and point it at the code. The camera will recognize the code and a notification will appear to open the encoded URL, contact or other data. Afterward, you can access the data in your browser or other apps. Additionally, you can install an app from the Google Play Store to make scanning QR codes easier.

Scanning QR codes is becoming increasingly common for Android users. With this guide, you will learn how to use your Android phone camera to scan a QR code in a few easy steps.
