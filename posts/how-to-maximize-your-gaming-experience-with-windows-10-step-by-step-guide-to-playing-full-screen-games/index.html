Before you can start playing full screen games on Windows 10, you need to make sure your system meets the minimum requirements. Additionally, it is beneficial to upgrade your system’s GPU or graphics card in order to have the best gaming performance. All of these components will help you maximize your gaming experience.

Next, you need to install the latest Windows 10 system update. This will ensure that your games run with the latest updates and patches, as well as provide additional security for your system. To do this, simply navigate to the Settings menu in the Windows 10 home screen and click on the “Update & Security” tab. Once you are on the page, simply click “Check for updates” and wait for the update process to finish.

Another important step is to adjust the settings in the Windows 10 game bar for the best gaming experience. By default, some game settings are set to low, so it’s important to adjust these depending on your system specs. To access the game bar settings, head to Start > Settings > Gaming > Game Bar. Once inside the game bar settings, you can manually adjust the settings for each game that you play.

Finally, you should also consider using a game boosting software to maximize your gaming experience. There are many game optimizers available that can help you get the best performance out of your system, making your gaming experience faster, smoother, and more enjoyable.

Q3: What is Windows HD Color and how do I enable it? A3: Windows HD Color is a feature that allows you to enjoy richer, more vibrant colors when playing games on Windows 10. To enable Windows HD Color, open the Settings app, go to the Display section, and turn on the Windows HD Color settings.

Q4: How can I play my games in full screen mode? A4: To play your games in full screen mode, press the Windows + G keys to open up the Xbox Game Bar. Then, click on the full-screen icon at the top-right corner of the window. You can also press the Alt + Enter keys to toggle between windowed and full screen mode.
