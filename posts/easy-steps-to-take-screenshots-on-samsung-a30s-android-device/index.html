1. Enabling screenshot shortcut from the device settings#

Taking screenshots on your Samsung A30s Android device can be easily done with just a few steps. First, enable the screenshot shortcut from the device settings. To do so, go to Settings, then select Advanced Features. Enable Smart Capture, then exit the settings. After that, on the home screen, swipe down from the top of the screen to open the Notification Panel. Tap the Screenshot icon, and you are good to go. Besides that, you can also take a screenshot by pressing the power and volume down buttons at the same time. Once you have taken the screenshot, a notification will pop up to give you access to share the image or delete it. With these easy steps, you can now take screenshots on your Samsung A30s Android device with ease.

2. How to Take a Screenshot on Android Samsung AO3S#

Enabling screenshot shortcut from the device settings is one of the simplest ways to take screenshots on your Android Samsung AO3S. To do this, go to your device’s Settings and tap on System. In the System menu, select the Gestures option. Then, in the Gestures menu, tap on Motions and Gestures where you will find an option to Take a Screenshot. Once enabled, you can take a screenshot by swiping your three fingers down on the screen. This is an easy way to quickly take screenshots without having to press multiple buttons.

Enabling a screenshot shortcut on your Android device is easy and can be done straight from the device settings. Open your device’s Settings>Advanced features and tap the toggle to enable Smart capture. Once enabled, you can take a screenshot by swiping your hand across the screen or pressing the power button and volume down at the same time. You can also take scrolling screenshots by tapping the Capture more button which appears after you take a screenshot. You’ll be able to capture the entire content of the page in one continuous image. That’s how to enable and use the Android screenshot shortcut on Samsung AO3S.

2. Taking screenshots using physical buttons#

Taking screenshots using physical buttons on a Samsung A30s Android device is an easy task. To capture your device’s screen, press and hold the Power button and Volume down button simultaneously. Release the buttons once you hear the shutter sound. The screenshot is automatically saved in the Screenshots folder in the Gallery app. To view the screenshot, open the Gallery app and go to the Albums tab. Tap the Screenshots folder and you will see all the screenshots you have taken. Alternatively, you can also view the screenshot from the notification panel after taking it. Just pull down the notification panel and tap the screenshot notification.

3. Editing and sharing screenshots#

Taking screenshots on Samsung A30s Android device can be a quick and convenient way to capture your favorite moments. Thankfully, it’s easy to do. The following guide will walk you through the steps of taking and sharing a screenshot on this device.

First, press and hold the Volume down button and the Power button simultaneously. When the screen flashes, it means the screenshot has been taken. To check the screenshot, go to the Gallery app and the screenshot will be found in the Screenshots album.

To share the screenshot, tap the Share icon, select the sharing method, and choose the app or person you want to share it with. Once you have selected the appropriate contacts, tap the Send button.

Finally, you can also edit the screenshot before you share it. To do this, open the screenshot from the Gallery app, tap the Edit icon, and use the built-in tools to customize the image. When you are satisfied with the result, tap the Save icon and share the edited screenshot.

With these easy steps, you can now take and share screenshots on your Samsung A30s Android device.
