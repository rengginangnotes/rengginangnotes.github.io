2. Activating the Scanner Feature#

Activating the Scanner Feature on your Android phone is the first step to scanning documents. To begin, find the ‘Settings’ icon on your home screen and open it. Scroll down until you see ‘Camera’ and tap on it to open. Turn on the ‘Scan documents’ option and tap ‘OK’ to confirm. After that, the scanner feature will be activated on your phone. Now you’re ready to begin scanning documents.

1. Enabling the Scanner Feature#

Activating the Scanner Feature on an Android phone is an easy task. All you need to do is access the camera app on your Android phone. Once you have activated the camera app, simply switch it to Scanner Mode, and you’ll be able to take a picture of a document or photo to upload and save. After you have taken the photo of the document, your photos will be automatically converted to a PDF format. To find the PDF files, go to the Gallery app on your Android phone and select the ‘Scanned’ folder. Your PDF files should be there. With this, you can now scan any document from the convenience of your Android phone.
