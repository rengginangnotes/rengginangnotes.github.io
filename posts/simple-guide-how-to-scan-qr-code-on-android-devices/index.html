Q: What is a QR code? A: A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned by many devices including smartphones and tablets. It can contain text, a URL, or other data.

Q: How do I scan a QR code on my Android device? A: To scan a QR code on Android, open the Camera app, point the camera at the code, and then tap the magnifying glass icon. The QR code should be recognized automatically and you will be able to view the encoded information.

Q: What should I do if my camera doesn’t recognize a QR code? A: If your camera does not recognize the QR code, try enabling the device’s flashlight and pointing it at the code. You can also try downloading a third-party QR code scanning app to scan the code.

Q: Can I generate a QR code on my Android device? A: Yes, there are several apps available for Android devices which allow you to generate QR codes. You can search the Google Play store for “QR code generator” to find a suitable app.

Q: What information can I store in a QR code? A: You can store any kind of information in a QR code, including text, URLs, Wi-Fi networks, contact information, photos, videos, and more. However, the amount of information that can be stored in a single QR code is limited.
