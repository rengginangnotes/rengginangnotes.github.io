• Tips and Tricks for Backing Up Contacts on iPhone#

Backing up contacts on your iPhone is important, especially if you’re thinking about transferring them to your Android tablet. Luckily, Apple makes it easy to export your iPhone contacts as a VCF file. Then, you can quickly and easily import them into your Android tablet. Here’s how to transfer your iPhone contacts to your Android tablet in just a few simple steps.

First, make sure your iPhone is running the latest version of iOS. Then, open the Phone app on your iPhone and head over to Contacts. Tap on the hamburger menu and select “Export vCard”. You’ll now be prompted to select which contacts you wish to export. Once that’s done, tap “Done” and the contacts will be exported to your device.

Next, connect your Android tablet to your computer using a USB cable. Then, transfer the exported VCF file to your Android device. Once that’s done, open the Contacts app on your Android tablet. From the main menu, select “Settings” and then select “Import Contacts”. Select the VCF file you just transferred from your iPhone and your contacts will now be imported to your Android tablet.

Finally, if you want to sync your contacts across both devices, you’ll need to use a cloud storage service. This will allow you to keep your contacts in sync across both devices. Simply log into the cloud storage service on your iPhone and Android tablet, and then select the contacts you wish to sync. Now your contacts will stay in sync no matter which device you’re using.

Following these simple steps, you can easily transfer contacts from your iPhone to your Android tablet. Now you can have access to all your contacts no matter where you are.
