Step 1: Enable USB Debugging on both your Android phone and tablet. USB debugging is a development feature that allows you to copy data between your phone and computer. To enable it on your Android phone, go to ‘Settings > System > Developer Options’ and toggle on ‘USB Debugging’. On your Android tablet, you can also enable USB debugging by tapping ‘Settings > System > Developer options’ and toggling on ‘USB Debugging’.

Step 2: Connect your Android phone and tablet to your computer with a USB cable. Once both devices are connected, your computer will recognize them as separate drives. From there, you can easily transfer files between the two devices, simply by dragging and dropping the files from one drive to the other.
