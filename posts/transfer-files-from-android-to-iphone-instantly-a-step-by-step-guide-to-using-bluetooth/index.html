1. Overview of Bluetooth Technology#

Bluetooth technology is a convenient way to transfer files between two devices, such as an Android and iPhone. With the simple pairing of Bluetooth wireless protocol, you can transfer files from your Android device to an iPhone instantly. Follow these steps to transfer files from an Android device to an iPhone using Bluetooth technology.

First, make sure that Bluetooth is enabled on both devices. On the iPhone, go to Settings > Bluetooth and toggle the switch to on. On the Android, go to Settings > Bluetooth and toggle the switch to on.

Once the Bluetooth is enabled on both devices, you’ll need to pairing the devices. On the iPhone, select the device you want to pair and tap connect. On the Android, select the device you want to pair and tap pair.

Once paired, a Bluetooth icon will appear on both devices. On the iPhone, go to the File Sharing app and select the files you want to transfer. Then, select the Send button. The files will be transferred to the Android device. On the Android, go to the Bluetooth Transfer app and select the files you want to receive. Then, select the Receive button. The files will be transferred to the iPhone.

Using Bluetooth technology, you can quickly and easily transfer files between an Android and an iPhone. Follow these simple steps to make your file transfer a breeze.
