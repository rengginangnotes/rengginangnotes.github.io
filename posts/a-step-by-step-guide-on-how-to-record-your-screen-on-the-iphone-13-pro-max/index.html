Q1: What are the requirements for recording your screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max?

A1: In order to record your screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max, you will need iOS 14 or above and an iPhone 13 Pro Max. Additionally, you will need a Mac running macOS 11 or higher.

Q2: How do I access the screen recorder on the iPhone 13 Pro Max?

A2: You can access the screen recorder on the iPhone 13 Pro Max by going to Settings > Control Center > Customize Controls > Screen Recording. Here, you can add the Screen Recording control to your Control Center.

Q3: How do I start recording my screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max?

A3: To start recording your screen, you can open the Control Center by swiping down from the top-right corner of your screen or swiping up from the bottom of your screen. Locate the Screen Recording icon and press it to begin the recording.

Q4: How do I record audio while recording my screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max?

A4: To record audio while recording your screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max, go to Settings > Control Center > Customize Controls and enable “Microphone in Screen Recording.” Once enabled, you will be able to record audio with your screen recording.

Q5: How do I stop recording my screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max?

A5: To stop recording your screen on the iPhone 13 Pro Max, you can either press the red status bar at the top of your screen, swipe up to open the Control Center and press the Screen Recording icon again, or press the Stop button on QuickTime after the recording has been transferred to your Mac.
