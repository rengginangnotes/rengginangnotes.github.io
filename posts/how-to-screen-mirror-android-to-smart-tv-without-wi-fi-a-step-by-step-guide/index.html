Q2. What types of Android devices support screen mirroring?

A2. Most Android devices support screen mirroring, including smartphones, tablets, and some laptops. Your device must have the Cast feature built-in or be connected to a compatible device that supports mirroring.

A3. You will need a compatible Android device, a smart TV with the Advanced Television System Committee (ATSC) tuner, and a High-Definition Multimedia Interface (HDMI) cable.

A4. No, an internet connection is not required for screen mirroring. However, some apps may require you to connect to the internet in order to use their features.
