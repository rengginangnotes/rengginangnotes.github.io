Turn on iCloud Backup and press “Back Up Now” to begin the process. Once the backup is complete, you can move on to the next step.

The second step is to download Google Drive on your iPhone. Once it’s installed, open the app and sign in to your Google account. Finally, you can transfer the data from your Google Drive account to your Android device. Download the Google Drive app to your Android device, open it, and sign in with your Google account.
