Before you begin the process, back up your WhatsApp data on your Android device. On your Android device, open WhatsApp and click on the menu button in the top right corner. Then, select “Settings” and tap “Chats.” At the bottom of the screen, select “Chat Backup” and tap “Back Up.”

Next, install WhatsApp on your iPhone 13 Pro Max, and open the app. In the opening page, choose to restore backup data from an Android device. Enter the same phone number you used for the Android device, and all your WhatsApp data will be transferred to the new iPhone.

If you want to transfer photos and videos, simply enable the feature in the settings menu. Next, you need to transfer the backed up WhatsApp data from the Android device to your iPhone 13 Pro Max. To do that, you need to make sure that both devices are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Finally, launch WhatsApp on your iPhone 13 Pro Max, and tap on the Restore option. This will restore all your previous WhatsApp data from the backed-up file. That’s it! With these steps, you can easily transfer your WhatsApp data from an Android to an iPhone 13 Pro Max.
